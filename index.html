About Sunita Nurses Bureau

Providing compassionate, professional, and personalized home healthcare services in Mumbai for over a decade. Your family's health is our priority.

Our Journey of Care

Founded in 2015 by Sunita Shankar Rathod, Sunita Nurses Bureau began with a simple vision: to bring quality healthcare to the comfort of people's homes. What started as a small team of dedicated nurses has grown into one of Mumbai's most trusted home healthcare providers.

With over a decade of experience, we've served more than 500 families across Mumbai, providing everything from critical ICU care at home to compassionate elderly care and specialized postnatal services.

"Our mission is to ensure that every patient receives the dignity, respect, and professional care they deserve, surrounded by the comfort of their loved ones and familiar environment."
